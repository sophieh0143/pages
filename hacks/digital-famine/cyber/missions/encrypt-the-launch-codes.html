Mission 3

Shield the Launch Codes

The alien fleet is scanning for plaintext keys. Hash every launch code with SHA-256 before the countdown expires.

Quick Objectives

  • Queue incoming launch codes for hashing.
  • Convert every code to SHA-256 digests.
  • Verify hashed output before transmitting to the defense grid.
